About Us

Our journey at BridgeNET Fiber began right here in Statesville, North Carolina. Our roots run deep, and our story starts with our humble beginnings as telephone workers three generations ago.

Our commitment to this community has never wavered. We’re not just another service provider; we are an integral part of your community. Over the years, we’ve grown alongside the city. Many of our team members proudly call Statesville their home, and they are dedicated to ensuring that every resident and business in our founding city has access to the essential fiber-optic technology required in today’s fast-paced digital landscape.

Yet, our vision extends far beyond the boundaries of Statesville. While we continue to nurture our roots at home, we also set our sights on the broader horizon. Our journey has only just begun, and we are ready to expand our footprint, bringing our state-of-the-art fiber network to new communities. We envision a future where high-speed internet isn’t a luxury but a fundamental service accessible to all, regardless of their location.

At BridgeNET Fiber, we are more than just a service provider; we are partners in progress, catalysts for growth, and champions of innovation, rooted in our construction heritage and committed to building a connected world for all.
